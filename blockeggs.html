Blockeggs

Step into the world of Blockeggs, a thrilling hardcore platformer where every jump, block, and climb brings you closer to victory — or one step away from starting over! Available for Android and iOs
Can you reach the top? Drop pentomino-shaped blocks and place them smartly to build your own path, jumping and climbing safely without falling.
Every level is unique, with randomly generated layouts, ensuring no two runs are the same.
As you progress, the game ramps up the difficulty with icy slopes, lava pits, creepy enemies, and shifting platforms that will push your reflexes and planning to the limit.
With each victory comes a shot at the jackpot mini-game, where you can unlock up to 35 unique skins to personalize your egg!
Are you ready to rise to the challenge and take your egg to the top?"

There are several power-ups to boost your ascent and help you overcome each level:

  • Pickaxe: Remove any blocks you’ve placed that no longer fit your strategy.
  • Bomb: Clear the entire level. A perfect reset button when you need a fresh start.
  • Grappling hook: Perform a massive 45° leap to reach distant platforms.
  • Rocket: Launch upward with a fast, powerful vertical jump.

Both the grappling hook and rocket come at a coin cost, so choose wisely. Will you use a power-up or save your coins for later?
The quicker you complete each level, along with collecting more coins, and minimizing deaths and dropped blocks, will lead to a higher star score at the end.
A higher star score unlocks additional opportunities in the jackpot, increasing your chances of winning new amazing skins!
The game is free to play, and it features only non-intrusive ads that are completely optional, which provide extra tries in the final jackpot or allow you to reroll duplicate skins.
